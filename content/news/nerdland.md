## Exciting news!
 
🎙️ Nerdland Podcast will be joining us at the very first edition of Brain Bee Belgium! 
 
Natha Kerkhofs, Peter Berx and Bart Van Peer from Nerdland will join us during the competition as part of our jury and will take on the role of question masters in the final round.

A huge thank you to the Nerdland team for supporting science outreach and education in Belgium. We cannot wait to welcome you!
